Contractor Lead Marketplace SaaS

ConstraCRM is a contractor lead marketplace and buyer management platform built for lead sellers, contractor networks, and growth-focused service businesses. Capture demand, organize buyer requests, and route contractor inquiries into a CRM-powered intake workflow.

Why ConstraCRM

Lead Intake

Capture contractor buyer requests through a clean intake portal built for scalable lead operations.

CRM Workflow

Route every submission into a structured pipeline for review, qualification, and follow-up.

Market Filtering

Organize inquiries by trade, target market, and service radius for smarter lead distribution.

Buyer Intake Portal

Request access to contractor leads by submitting your company information, preferred service category, target market, and delivery preferences. Your request will be added to the ConstraCRM pipeline for review.

Future SaaS upgrades can include email alerts, SMS notifications, login access, subscriptions, territory routing, and database-backed lead management.
